Plant Doctor

Improve the sustainability of the agriculture industry by allowing farmers to be able to identify disease or malnourishments in their plants and crops. By using image recognition software, we hope to decrease the number of crops lost to harmful diseases and infestations, and to help large farmers and small gardeners alike to protect their plants.

Features

1. Upload

Upload a picture of any plant that could potentially be diseased.

2. Detect

Our image recognition model will determine what kind of disease your plant might have.

3. Treat

Get your search results and recieve educated insights on how to treat your plant!
